Your service starts with our 7-Point Pet Care Check,* a quick screening to make sure nothing looks or feels abnormal on your pet.
We personalize your dog’s service to their needs, which may include a thorough brushoff or initial shave if your dog has a thick coat.
Here’s where the real transformation happens. Our stylists work their magic to create personalized cuts by breed and style preferences.
We gently massage shampoo into your dog’s coat to remove dirt, oil and debris. We use a variety of personalized drying methods to ensure your dog’s safety and comfort, from air- and towel-drying to non-heated hand-held dryers.
Your pet’s overall health is our top priority, so we’ll send them home with a full report detailing services provided and aftercare recommendations to help keep them healthy and looking fantastic until their next grooming appointment.

FAQ

  • What is the price for a dog bath or bath with haircut?
    You can call your local grooming salon before your visit to receive an estimated price. Based on your preferred grooming service options, you can discuss details over the phone or in person when you meet your groomer at the start of your pet's groom.
  • How do I get my pet started with a grooming service?
    For dogs, we offer online booking on our home page. For cats, please call your salon to confirm if a groomer for cats is available. If you'd like to chat with a groomer about your pet's specific needs, call your local salon.
  • How do I change or cancel an appointment?
    Manage your appointments through your account.
  • How is safety ensured for all dog grooming services?
    We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of animal care and safety in the industry, as developed under the supervision of our Director of Veterinary Medicine, with counsel from several independent experts in animal care, behavior and ethics. A few ways we ensure your pet's safety in the grooming salon is by: Completing a comprehensive 7-Point Pet Care Check for every pet upon check-in to ensure nothing looks or feels abnormal Using ramps or steps for senior dogs to get into the tubs or bathe really large dogs on the floor Providing industry-standard comfortable and safe kennels Utilizing the Groomer's Helper tool, designed to help safely maintain control of your pet's body and head while on the grooming table
